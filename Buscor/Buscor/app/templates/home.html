UMP BUSCOR WEB APPLICATION

Buscor (Pty) Ltd shares the vision of the Department of Transport to become a world-class bus service company, offering a first-class transport system for Mpumalanga Province. This system is planned with the community, industry, and labor, ensuring integration, reliability, affordability, and safety.

As a responsible operator, Buscor works with the Department and all relevant stakeholders to achieve these goals. Our motto is clear: Passenger safety is our passion.

AS students of the University of Mpumalanga (UMP) We are thrilled to announce our integration with Buscor , allowing students to purchase tickets from the Buscor website. These tickets are valid for taking them to various places around Mbombela City, including routes from the bus station to the University of Mpumalanga.
